What makes a strong password?

A strong password contains the following:

  • At least one uppercase letter.
  • At least one lowercase letter.
  • At least one number.
  • At least one symbol.
  • A length of at least twelve characters.

Even though password length is more important than password complexity, it is still imperative that you make your password more complex so that it would be more difficult for your account to get compromised.
